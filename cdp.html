Colina da Penha

The first chapel was founded in 1622 by the crew and passengers of a ship which had narrowly escaped capture by the Dutch. The chapel served as a point of pilgrimage for sailors embarking on a hazardous voyage. The chapel was completely rebuilt, along with the Bishop' s Palace in 1837. Dur to it's beautiful and romantic sencery, it has became a famous spot for pre wedding photo taking.
